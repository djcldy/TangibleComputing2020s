Cyberpunk
Keeping the motifs and aesthetics of cyberpunk incooperated into a conceptual time sketch.


10 Visual Elements
Sharp angles,
Bright Neon Lights,
High levels of contrast,
Purple undertones,
Blue/Pink highlights,
Focal Points in the centre,
Grids,
Blured elements in the background,
Repeated patterns,
Decay.


10 Interaction
Floating elements,
Static background,
Layered movement,
Blurred movement,
One point perspective movement (in a line),
Hyper-Futuristic dynamic movement,
Rectangular,
Sharp movement,
Goth motifs,
Single focus point (movement follows that point).
