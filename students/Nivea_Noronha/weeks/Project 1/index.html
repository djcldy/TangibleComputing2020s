Project One Decoding Cultures of Time-based Media

Summary

For week 2 we had to choose an aesthetic culture of our choice for our project and research the visual elements and interaction dynamics that made it what it is. We also had to write a summary of of what our chosen aesthetic is along with our research. We also learned about what functions are and how to use them. With this knowledge we were asked to loop a function 10000¬¬¬ times to create different patterns for each day of the week. We learned how to make various shapes witht the functions and how to position , fill and add a stroke color to them.
